Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 10, 2026

Overdue Radar is a private custom integration built for a single bookkeeping client. This page explains how data is handled for that integration.

Data collected

The integration may access accounting data required to monitor overdue invoices, including invoice details, customer names, due dates, balances due, and related summary metadata.

How data is used

Data is used only to generate internal monitoring views and daily summaries for the authorised client organisation. Data is not sold or shared for advertising.

AI usage

Data accessed through this integration is not used to train, fine-tune, adapt, or enhance AI or machine learning models.

Data retention

Only the minimum data needed to operate the integration is retained. Data is stored for operational reporting and may be deleted on client request or when the integration is discontinued.

Security

Reasonable technical and organisational measures are used to protect credentials and client data, including secure transport, restricted access, and secret management practices.
